🌍 About Nyelva Map

Welcome to Nyelva Map, an interactive linguistic atlas designed to help you explore the complexity of Earth's languages. This map takes an intuitive approach—mapping major languages based on their core origins or where they are dominantly spoken today. Because the dividing line between a language and a dialect is fluid, this map is an educational tool rather than an absolute geopolitical boundary.

🎨 Visual Framework

  • Colors: Every marker and area is color-coordinated to represent its Major Language Family. Clicking a point highlights its entire geographical footprint.
  • Sizing Hierarchy:
    • Large: Massive global linguistic hubs (e.g., American English, Brazilian Portuguese).
    • Medium: Standard primary national languages.
    • Small: Localized regional languages or dense minority dialects.
  • Information: Click any interactive diamond to view its ancestry, approximate speaker counts, writing script, and a direct link to its Wikipedia article.
